About

Tape Runs Out formed in Cambridge, UK in 2012. On March 30th 2015 they released a double a-side called Friends/Flowers on Ear to Ear Records. They followed this with their Knife EP, released on October 1st 2015.

"A polished mature sound, rife with hooks, addictive vocals, innocence and optimism" - The Record Stache
