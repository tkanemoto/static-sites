a shoegazey/indie-ish band from cambridge, uk

 

"Spectral, shoegazey, magical" - Fierce Panda

About

Formed in Cambridge, UK in 2012, Tape Runs Out are Liam Goodrum-Bell (vocals, guitar, synth), Yasmin Prebble (keyboard), Takeshi Kanemoto (bass), Rich Barker (guitar), Ellie Winter (hammered dulcimer, vocals) and Clare Myerscough (violin, synth, vocals).

Previous Next

 

On March 30th 2015 they released a double a-side called Friends/Flowers on Ear to Ear Records. They followed this with their Knife EP, released on October 1st 2015.

"A polished mature sound, rife with hooks, addictive vocals, innocence and optimism" - The Record Stache
